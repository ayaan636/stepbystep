About Thailand

Thailand is a multicultural nation that's renowned for its lovely beaches, culture and delectable street food. Ranging from the congested roads of Bangkok to the serene temples of Chiang Mai, it has something for everyone. The Thai are friendly and hospitable and their festivals like Songkran and Loy Krathong to make it better for the visitors

How To Get There

Abu Dhabi (AUH) or Dubai (DXB), fly to Tokyo (Haneda/Narita) or Osaka (Kansai). Emirates, Etihad, or Japan Airlines have direct flights and indirect flights. The cities are well linked with Japan domestic flights and trains and can be reached within a short span of time.

When To Go There

Spring (March-May) for cherry blossoms, autumn (September-November) for stunning foliage. Winter is the best time for snow sports, and summer for colorful festivals.

Things To See There

Tokyo’s futuristic skyline, Kyoto’s ancient temples, Mount Fuji’s breathtaking views, Osaka’s delicious street food, Nara’s friendly deer, and Hiroshima’s historic peace memorial.
